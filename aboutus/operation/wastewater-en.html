BLCP Power Plant Wastewater Management
BLCP Power Plant has a wastewater treatment system capable of handling approximately 1,700 cubic meters per day, divided into 2 main parts:

•Wastewater from power generation process: 1,500 cubic meters/day

•Wastewater from employees and general activities: 200 cubic meters/day
