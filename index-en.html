BLCP Power Plant operates electricity generation business
using modern production equipment and technology combined with
strict environmental management to comply with government standards and to consider
the feelings and needs of the community as a priority.
BLCP Power Plant uses high-quality bituminous coal as fuel, mostly imported
from Australia, with a total generation capacity of 1,434 megawatts.
